Quick Bites

Pav Bhaji

Ingredients: Potatoes (boiled and mashed), Mixed vegetables {e.g., peas, carrots, cauliflower} (finely chopped), Butter, Onion (finely chopped), Tomatoes, (finely chopped), Pav Bhaji masala, Red chili powder, Salt, Lemon wedges, Coriander leaves (chopped), Pav (bread rolls), Butter for serving

Preparation:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add finely chopped onions. Sauté until translucent.
  2. Add finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and cook until they are soft.
  4. Add mashed potatoes, pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  5. Mash the mixture using a potato masher until smooth.
  6. Add water if required to adjust the consistency.
  7. Cook for a few more minutes until the flavors blend well.
  8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and a dollop of butter.
  9. Heat pav on a tawa (griddle) with butter until lightly toasted. That's it, Serve hot pav bhaji with a lemon wedge and additional butter on the side.

Veggie

Aloo Fry

Ingredients: Potatoes, Oil, Salt, Turmeric, Red chili powder, Cumin seeds, Coriander leaves (for garnish)

Preparation:

  1. Peel and chop the potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them crackle.
  3. Add chopped potatoes to the pan and sauté on medium heat.
  4. Sprinkle salt, turmeric, and red chili powder over the potatoes. Mix well.
  5. Cook until the potatoes are golden brown and crispy, stirring occasionally.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves before serving.

Meaty Marvels

Tandoori Fish

Ingredients: Fish fillets (any white fish) - 500g, Yogurt - 1/2 cup, Lemon juice - 2 tbsp, Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp, Tandoori masala - 2 tbsp, Red chili powder - 1 tsp, Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp, Salt to taste, Oil for brushing

Preparation:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, tandoori masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt to form a marinade
  2. Marinate fish fillets in the mixture for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F) and grease a baking tray.
  4. Place marinated fish fillets on the tray and brush with oil.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until fish is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken (boneless) - 500g, Butter - 4 tbsp, Onion (finely chopped) - 1 large, Tomato (pureed) - 3 large, Ginger-garlic paste - 2 tbsp, Green chilies (chopped) – 2, Cashew nuts - 1/4 cup, Fresh cream - 1/2 cup, Garam masala - 1 tsp, Red chili powder - 1 tsp, Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp, Salt to taste, Fresh coriander leaves (chopped) - for garnish

Preparation:

  1. Marinate chicken pieces with yogurt, turmeric powder, and salt. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat butter in a pan, add chopped onions, and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato puree, garam masala, red chili powder, and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  6. Add ground cashew nuts and fresh cream, simmer for a few minutes.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
